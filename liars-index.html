How to Play 📜

🏆 GOAL: SURVIVAL

  • Dice = Lives. You want to KEEP them.
  • Lose a round = Lose a die.
  • Last player standing wins.

👀 THE BID

Guess the TOTAL number of dice in the WHOLE ROOM (e.g., "Five 2s").

👉 YOUR TURN (Version Logic)

You must bet a Higher Version Number than the last player.

  • 1. Major Update (Quantity): Increase the quantity (e.g. "Three" -> "Four"). "Four 2s" beats "Three 6s"
  • 2. Minor Update (Face Value): Same quantity, higher face (e.g. "2s" -> "5s"). "Three 5s" beats "Three 2s"
  • OR... CALL LIAR! 🤥 If you think they don't exist.
